# Company Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year.
Leave must be applied at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal.
Sick leave is separate and capped at 10 days per year with a medical certificate.
Maternity leave is 26 weeks as per company policy.

# Finance Guidelines

All expense claims above $500 must be approved by the Finance Manager.
Reimbursements are processed on the last working day of each month.
Travel expenses require original receipts and must be submitted within 7 days.

# General Company Info

The company was founded in 2010 and operates across 5 countries.
Our headquarters is in Mumbai, India.
All employees must complete compliance training by end of Q1 each year.
